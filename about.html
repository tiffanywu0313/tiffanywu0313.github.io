A little about me.

Hi there! I'm Tiffany, an aspiring product designer currently studying Computer Science student at the University of British Columbia. Although I still enjoy coding when the occasion arises, I chose to pursue design after realizing that I was more interested in why we build software and for whom.

What really drew me in to design was being able to stand at the meeting point of technology and the humanities (such as art, typography, and psychology). I love how I get to be curious all the time when working on projects; whether it be interviewing users, identifying paint points, or analyzing user data, there's always something new to learn. My goal is to pursue projects that will improve the lives of everyday people -- be it through design work or otherwise.

In my free time, I like to play volleyball, ultimate, and dance. I also love hanging out with friends, making food, and coming up with my next creative project.
