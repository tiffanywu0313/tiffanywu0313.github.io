Hi, I'm Tiffany!

I am a Product Designer who strives to solve problems for people with a human-centered and data-driven approach. I want to create products and experiences that make life easier one little interaction at a time.

I'm a self-taught designer who recently graduated from the University of British Columbia with a BSc in Computer Science. Currently interested in Early Career Product or UX Design opportunities.

What initially led me to digital product design was being able to stand at the meeting point of technology and art (e.g. design, typography, and psychology). What I like most about design now, is getting to be curious when learning from users through research and empathetic when identifying paint points and designing new solutions. My goal is to pursue projects that will improve the lives of everyday people.
